About

Automatic Data Processing, Inc. (ADP) is an American provider of human resources management software and services, headquartered in Roseland, New Jersey.

At ADP, what we do is about people. Although we have a strong history of providing solutions for human resource challenges, we strive to do more than that. We challenge ourselves to anticipate, think forward and take action in a way that empowers us to shape the changing world of work. For over 75 years, we’ve led the way in defining the future of business solutions. ADP is proud to be named the 2024 Fortune® World's Most Admired Companies™ list again for 18 consecutive years*.

History

ADP was founded in 1949 by Henry Taub as Automatic Payrolls, Inc., a manual payroll processing business. The company was among the first to leverage computer technology to automate business processes, a move that ultimately shaped its future.

Global Reach

Headquartered in Roseland, New Jersey, ADP operates in more than 140 countries, serving over 860,000 businesses worldwide. Its global reach ensures it can handle payroll and HR needs for multinational corporations.
